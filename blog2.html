What is hacktoberfest and why you should care ?

Hacktoberfest is an open-source contribution challenge that takes place every year in October . The goal of the event is to encourage people to contribute to open-source projects by making at least five contributions before the end of the month . In return, participants can receive a free Hacktoberfest t-shirt as an incentive .

Participating in Hacktoberfest is a great way to give back to the open-source community and contribute to projects that you're passionate about. It's an opportunity to learn new skills, collaborate with others, and make meaningful contributions to the software that powers our world. By contributing to open-source, you can help improve the quality and reliability of software, and make it more accessible to people around the globe. Overall, if you care about open-source, Hacktoberfest is an event you should definitely consider participating in.
